10. Tips for Cambridge C1

  • Use linking words to connect ideas smoothly.
  • Avoid repetition by using synonyms.
  • Be concise in your answers but provide enough detail.
  • Practice idioms and phrasal verbs to sound more natural.
  • Plan your writing before starting to ensure coherence.
